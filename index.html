About Us

About Us

As Hidroist, Our intention is to use hydrogen as the main energy, next to wind and solar energy, in turkey. We want our country to produce their own vehicles running with hydrogen as fuel to contribute with the country’s economy as well. Hidroist was created in 2011. Our Team has students from several departments such as the Electric and Electronic, Mechanical, Metallurgy and Materials, Chemical and Management department.

We got into different events in order to fulfill our purpose:

2012 we achieved the title “Prize for the best Style “on an event made by Tübitak. In 2014 and 2015 we reached the first place and received the title "Prize of Turkey Championship". In 2015 and 2016 we represented Istanbul University and our country in the best way we could at the Europe Shell-Eco Marathon and got 5th Place. In 2016 we got 1st place at the Turkey Shell-Eco Marathon Races and managed to achieve the Technical Innovation prize. In 2017 we got first place at the Shell-Eco Marathon Turkey Races once again.
